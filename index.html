Practices

Some of the skills we teach

Front Crawl

The front crawl calls for you to kick hard with your feet, while bringing your arms over your head and into the water one at a time.

Backstroke

The backstroke has many similarities to the front crawl, except that you float on your back, rather than propelling your front through the water first.

Breaststroke

The breaststroke uses a frog-style kick, where you will bend your knees and then kick your legs out beneath the water. Your arm stroke will begin with a sweep out from your breast and then a sweep in back to your breast.

Sidestroke

The stroke resembles picking apples, as you will float on your side, reach up like when picking an apple and then reach down like when putting the apple into a basket.
